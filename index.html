Cookies offer just the right amount of sweetness at the end of the night. chocolate chip with walnuts are my favorite kind. For me there is nothing better then sitting down for a relaxing night in with a glass of my favorite wine and a couple of cookies.

List of Ingredients

  • Lots of Love
  • A whole lot of fun
  • A little bit of patience

Instructions

  1. First you grab a large bag a cookies and a bottle of wine. Then set both aside next to the couch. Do Not forget the wine glasses!
  2. Then you put on your P.J.'s & grab your coziest pair of slippers and put them on your feet. This becomes especially important if you will be sharing the couch with anybody else.
  3. Last but certainly not least you need to go back to the couch, pour yourself a glass of wine, grab a handfull of cookies, and watch a movie on Netflix!
